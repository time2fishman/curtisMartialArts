FAQ

What style do you teach?

Hapkido, Kuk Sul Won, and American Kenpo. This is a unique blend of Martial Arts with an emphasis on self-defense.

Can I try a class before signing up?

Yes. You can try a full week (up to 3 days) for free.

How long is a class?

1 hour.

What do I wear to try class out?

Something comfortable. Sweats or shorts and a t-shirt works great.

How many classes can I attend?

As many as you'd like. Tuition covers unlimited classes. We offer up to 3 per week.

Is there sparring in class?

Yes. We spar about once per week. We believe sparring is essential for learning self defense.

Do you require contracts?

No. We are a pay-by-the month school.
